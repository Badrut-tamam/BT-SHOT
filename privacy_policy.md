# Privacy Policy for Bubble Shooter: Premium Edition

**Last Updated: May 6, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in our mobile application.

## 1. Data Collection
Bubble Shooter: Premium Edition **does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII)**. We do not require account registration or login.

## 2. Local Storage
The app uses local storage (SharedPreferences) on your device to store:
- Game progress (unlocked levels)
- High scores
- User preferences (sound/vibration settings)
- Virtual currency (coins) earned through gameplay

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

## 3. Third-Party Services
We may use standard Flutter plugins for local device functionality:
- **Audioplayers**: For sound effects.
- **Vibration**: For haptic feedback.

These services do not collect or transmit user data.

## 4. Children's Privacy
Our app is safe for all ages. We do not collect information from children or anyone else.

## 5. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.

## 6. Contact Us
If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@bt-shot.com.
